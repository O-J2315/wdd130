Purpose

"White Water Rafting is an incredible and fun outdoor activity to do with your friends, family and even alone. Get to know all the trips available and costs for reservations. All trips are guided and free to cancel anytime."

Audience

"Who are the target audience? Perfect for anyone aged +12 interested in outdoor activitie, who likes to trip and travel to have fun and excersice. What do they want? To find awesome and cool trips to enyoy white water rafting activity as a recreation or even vacation. Find places to make reservations easily. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? More information regarding the Cost, guides, locations and geographical characteristics of the available trips. How to make reservations and how to contact for help."
