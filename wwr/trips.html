Idaho, US

Home to the most navigable miles of whitewater in the lower 48 states, it’s no surprise that Idaho tops several lists when it comes to river sports. Embark on an adrenaline-pumping adventure and splash your way through the wild rapids of the iconic Middle Fork of the Salmon River. Or, take the family on an exciting trip through the deepest river gorge in the country. No matter your skill level, whether you’ve got five days to spend or just an afternoon.

Mamut, Slovenija

The breathtaking beauty of Croatia and its diversity have been world-famous for a long time! Experience enchanting history, untouched nature, crystal clear Adriatic Sea, warm hospitality of Croats, top cuisine, interesting traditions and culture with our experienced and professional local guides. This trip includes all the best that Croatia has to offer.

Otawa, Canada

Come challenge the rapids of the Ottawa River with your family, friends or other adventurers in the Canadian wilderness near Ottawa. Rafting Momentum offers you a rafting activity that will satisfy your desire to discover, adventure and share. It is a sporting activity that awaits you near the capital of Canada, in a relaxing, rejuvenating and exceptionally warm natural environment.

Cancun, Mexico

Yes! You can now do Rafting in Cancun. One of the most sought-after activities among adventurers in the whole world has finally reached Cancun. You will not only be taking on this adventure in the only white-water rapids in Cancun and the whole Yucatán Peninsula, but you’ll be doing it in the longest man-made circuit in the world!
