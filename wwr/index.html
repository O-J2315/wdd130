What is our Purpose?

The fresh mountain air, a picturesque landscape, physical activity, and numerous rapids are no wonder why this sport is such healthy exercise. It's purpose is to provide fun and exciting activity while enjoying of the nature around.

What do we offer?

Emerald green streams, rousing waves and riverscapes – the outdoor activity of white water rafting is ideal for all nature lovers and (soon-to-be) adventure sports enthusiasts. Rafting requires coordination and team spirit. That's why it offers an unfogettable experience.
